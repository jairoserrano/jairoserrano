**Assistant Professor at Universidad Tecnológica de Bolívar (UTB) | Tech Entrepreneur | Researcher**

I am a **PhD in Engineering (Systems and Computing)** from Pontificia Universidad Javeriana, with a Master's in Free Software from UOC and a Systems Engineering degree from UTB. Based in Cartagena, Colombia, I am passionate about bridging the gap between academia and the tech industry. I combine my teaching duties with technological entrepreneurship, driving innovation from the classroom to real-world applications.

### 🔭 What I Do
- **Teaching & Mentorship:** As an Assistant Professor at UTB, I train the next generation of engineers in systems architecture, AI, and software development. 
- **Scientific Research:** Active contributor to social simulation models, including work on real-time visualization for the **WellProdSim** social simulator.
- **Tech Entrepreneurship:** I design and implement technological solutions, integrating complex models into practical, scalable tools.

### 🌱 Current Focus & Interests
- **Agent-Based Social Simulation (ABSS):** Specializing in decision-making models, BDI (Belief-Desire-Intention) agents, and integration with **MicroLLMs**.
- **Photovoltaic Systems:** Monitoring, technological optimization, and solar plant integrations.
- **Artificial Intelligence & Architecture:** Machine Learning, FastAPI architectures, DevOps practices, and Blockchain (Hyperledger Fabric).
