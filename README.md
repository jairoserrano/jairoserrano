**Academic Director & Professor | Tech Entrepreneur | Researcher**

I am a **PhD in Engineering (Systems and Computing)** from Pontificia Universidad Javeriana, with a Master's in Free Software (UOC) and a Systems Engineering degree (UTB). Based in Cartagena, Colombia, I am passionate about bridging the gap between advanced academia, technology, and society. 

Currently, I serve as the **Coordinator of the Computing Emphasis for the Master's and PhD in Engineering** (a joint program between UTB and Universidad de Cartagena) and the **Director of the Software Engineering Specialization** at UTB. Furthermore, I am proudly leading the creation of the upcoming **Master's in Artificial Intelligence** at UTB. My academic journey also heavily involves pioneering technology-mediated education, particularly through my foundational work with UTB's Interactive Virtual Learning System (**SAVIO**).

### 🔭 What I Do
- **Academic Leadership:** Directing postgraduate programs (Specialization, Master's, and PhD levels) and shaping the future curriculum for Artificial Intelligence and Software Engineering in the region.
- **EdTech & Virtual Learning:** Extensive experience designing and implementing virtual education ecosystems. I have played a crucial role in the adoption of open-source tools and LMS platforms (like Moodle) to build scalable educational environments like **SAVIO**.
- **Scientific Research:** Active contributor to social simulation models, including work on real-time visualization for the **WellProdSim** social simulator.
- **Tech Entrepreneurship:** I design and implement technological solutions, driving innovation from the classroom to real-world, scalable applications.

### 🌱 Current Focus & Interests
- **Agent-Based Social Simulation (ABSS):** Specializing in decision-making models, BDI (Belief-Desire-Intention) agents, and integration with **MicroLLMs**.
- **Technology-Mediated Education:** Virtual learning systems, LMS architecture, **adaptive learning strategies**, **user profiling**, and open-source applications for both higher education and corporate training.
- **Photovoltaic Systems:** Monitoring, technological optimization, and solar plant integrations.
- **Artificial Intelligence & Architecture:** Machine Learning, FastAPI architectures, DevOps practices, and Blockchain (Hyperledger Fabric).
